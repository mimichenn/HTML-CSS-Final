About Us

Daily Delights specializes in delicious food featuring fresh ingredients and masterful preparation by our culinary team. Whether you’re ordering a multi-course meal or grabbing a drink and pizza at the bar, Daily Delight's lively, casual yet upscale atmosphere makes it perfect for dining with friends, family, clients and business associates. At Daily Delights, we’re serving up more than 500 customers each day. In fact, our Famous Salmon recipe is one of our unexpected specialties. Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than four decades, is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it’s topped with our signature herb-garlic butter, then carved tableside.
